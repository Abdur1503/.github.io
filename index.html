// About

From shipping code to shipping insight.

AR

I started as a full-stack engineer — almost three years building backend systems and microservices at Tata Research Development and Design Centre, where I learned how data is actually produced, stored, and broken in production systems. That turned out to be the best possible training for analytics: I don't just read dashboards, I understand what's happening upstream when a number looks wrong.

Today I work as a Data Analyst at Etisalat by e&, the UAE's leading telecom operator, where I use SQL, Snowflake, and Power BI to turn millions of customer interaction records into decisions — work that's helped cut repeat-caller volume by 40% and lift First Call Resolution by 30%.

Alongside that, I build independent machine learning projects end-to-end — from a deployed real-estate price predictor to healthcare and retail analytics case studies — because I'd rather demonstrate a skill than describe it. Currently based in Dubai on a transferable employment visa, and open to Data Analyst / Data Scientist roles across worldwide, with particular interest in healthcare and operations-heavy domains.
